Services

See What We Offer

    Screenprinting

  • Screenprinting is an affordable printing technique that can be applied to most any type of branded apparel or material. From work shirts to uniforms, ShowOffs can help you say more on your screen-printed apparel for maximum marketing impact. ShowOffs can help you determine the function and quality needed in your branded apparel so that you select the perfect type of screen-printed item for your needs. When you’re ready to order, all we need is a vector file (EPS, Illustrator, PDF or similar) of your logo, and we will work with you to ensure that your branded apparel or item turns out exactly as you wanted.

    • Embroidery

  • Embroidery creates a much higher-quality look than other screen-printed apparel. While more expensive than screen printed clothing, embroidery can give you a more professional, polished look for your corporate events and tradeshows. ShowOffs can work with your company to identify your needs and budget to determine the right look for your embroidered pieces.

    • Promotional Products

  • If you don’t want to settle for the same, tried-and-true promotional item, let us consult with you before you place your order. The ShowOffs team will work to understand your customer and promotional needs, and can help you identify unique items to consider for your branded gifts or merchandise. We’ll also help you balance those considerations with the quality and quantity of the promotional item needed, to give you maximum impact for your budget. Our ordering process is fairly simple – just provide us with the vector art (EPS, Illustrator, PDF or similar) and we’ll take it from there. You’ll have the opportunity to view a proof of your promotional product before we place your order. ShowOffs can even take care of shipping your promo products to a tradeshow or event for you if needed.
