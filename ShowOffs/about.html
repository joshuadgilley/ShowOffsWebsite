About Us

ShowOffs Story

ShowOffs is a family-owned and operated business located in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina. Owner Mary Lou Kennedy has lived and worked in the local marketplace in a graphic and marketing-related capacity for more than 20 years. Her husband, Steve, is originally from the Charleston area, and is a 1973 graduate of the Citadel. Before launching ShowOffs, the Kennedys operated a FastSigns franchise from 1991-2001 in the Charleston area. Mary Lou and Steve have two children: a son, Daniel, who graduated from Wando High School and lives/works in St. Augustine, FL, and a daughter, Madaline, who also graduated from Wando HS and helps her mother run the embroidery department at ShowOffs. Other ShowOffs team members include Bethany, Josh and Nick.
